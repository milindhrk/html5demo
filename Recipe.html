Quick Hummus Recipe

The recipe makes quick tasty hummus with no messing. It has been adapted from a number of different recipes.

Hummus is a thick paste used heavily in greek and middle eastern dishes. It is very tasty with salads, grilled meats and pitta breads

Ingredients

  • A 400g can of chick peas
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • half redpepper
  • a pinch of cayenne pepper
  • a clove of garlic
  • a dash of olive

Instructions

  1. Remove the skin from the garlic and chop coarsely.
  2. Remove all the seeds ad stalk from the pepper and chop coarsely.
  3. Add all ingredients into a food processor.
  4. Process all the ingredients into a paste.
  5. If you want a coarse"chunky" hummus, process it for a shorter time.
  6. If you want smoother hummus, process it for a longer duration of time.
    7. For different flavours, you could try blending a small measure and coriander, chili pepper, lime and chipotle, harissa and mint, or spinach and feta cheese.

Storage

Refrigerate the finished hummus in a sealed container. You shpuld be able tp use it for about as week after you've made it. If it starts to become fizzy, you must discard it.

Hummus is suitable for freezing:You should thaw it and use it within a couple of months
